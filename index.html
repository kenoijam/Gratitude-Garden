What is it?

Two gardens. One practice of gratitude.

Gratitude Garden is made up of two separate experiences. The first is a personal daily journal where you answer a few reflective prompts, and in return receive a symbolic flower dedicated just to you. Each species carries its own meaning, chosen based on the words and emotions in your entry.

The second is a shared community space where you can post a moment of gratitude and plant your own symbolic bloom for others to see. Together, these two gardens turn everyday feelings into something living, beautiful, and worth returning to.

The Personal Garden

Choose your mood from the button grid, pick what shaped your day, then add any extra thoughts in the journal field.

Garden wisdom

How to tend your garden

Do it anywhere

You do not need a quiet desk or a special routine. Reflect at lunch, on the bus, or before you sleep.

Be specific, not general

"The coffee tasted perfect today" grows a truer bloom than "I am grateful." The more specific, the richer the keywords.

Save your milestones

Use the Save PNG button at meaningful moments. One week, one month, one full season tells a story worth keeping.

Trust what you feel

The symbolic flower is chosen from your own words. Honest, unfiltered entries lead to the blooms that resonate most.
